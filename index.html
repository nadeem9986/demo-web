File Guidelines

What You Need to Know

  • Accepted File Types

    STL is most common. We also accept OBJ, 3MF, and AMF files. Maximum file size: 100MB.

  • Turnaround Time

    Most prints are ready in 2–4 working days. Larger or complex models may take longer.

  • Payment

    We contact you with a quote first. Pay only after you agree. UPI, bank transfer, or cash accepted.

  • Delivery or Pickup

    Chennai-local delivery available. You can also pick up from our studio directly.
